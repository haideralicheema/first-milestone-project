Weightlifting

This is a class designed to help you to gain or maintain muscle size or to increase your strength. We split this into powerlifting and olympic lifting and our classes are perfect for all levels, ranging from absolute newbies to experienced lifters! We have designed a unique system that allows people from all levels to participate and help one another to achieve their strength and lifting goals. Our powerlifting classes are on Tuesday and Saturday from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM and our olympic lifting classes are on Thursday and Sunday from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM.


Martial Arts

Whether you want to learn to defend yourself or if you thrive off the discipline aspect of this wonderful group of skills, we have fully qualified trainers ready to help you on your journey. No experience is necessary to join our classes, as our trainers are flexible enough to tailor the class to your specific needs. We cover a range of disciplines including grappling, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and kickboxing. Our classes run daily between 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM and we have a complete facility for practice outside of class times which includes a Martial Arts arena, heavy bags and sparring equipment.


Regular Exercise Classes

Boxersize, Spin and other bespoke classes. If you want to request a class, then our advisors are trained in many areas so please leave a message if you want to know what else can be organised.


Nutrition and Healthcare

Our nutritionists are constantly working to keep up to date with the most recent evidence-based nutritional advice. They form bespoke plans to help people to achieve their physique goals whilst maintaining optimal health. They run a class once a week on Mondays between 6:00 PM and 7:00 PM where they teach our members about wellbeing and nutrition. Once you enrol onto their program, you only need to check in once a month after your initial class for our advisors to check up on your progress. Book your first class now.


Spirituality and Wellbeing

Are you getting too stressed out with your routine? Do you struggle to find a work-life-balance? Do you feel the need to have more grounding in your life? Our spirituality facilities range from meditation and yoga to one-to-one counselling by trained advisors who are here to look after your mental and spiritual wellbeing. We firmly believe that all aspects of a persons life should be healthy and if somebody wants to make a change in their life but doesn't know where to start then this might be the perfect starting point! Our advisors are also equipped with a firm understading of physical health so we can work towards a full wellbeing solution for our members. Arrange your first session today.
